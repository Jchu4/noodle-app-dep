Stir-fry Zucchini Noodles

Chicken Category

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • yellow onions , spiralized
  • small zucchini , spiralized, patted dry with paper towel
  • tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
  • tablespoons low sodium teriyaki sauce
  • tablespoon sesame seeds

Preparation Time

  • 10min

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and cook for 4 to 5 minutes, or until translucent and tender.
  3. Add soy sauce, teriyaki sauce and sesame seeds; mix and continue to cook for 5 minutes, or until zucchini is tender.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Serve.
